Recipe 1: Spaghetti Carbonara
Ingredients:
- Spaghetti
- Guanciale
- Eggs
- Pecorino Romano
- Black Pepper
Steps:
1. Cook spaghetti.
2. Fry guanciale.
3. Whisk eggs with pecorino.
4. Combine pasta with guanciale and egg mixture off-heat.
5. Season with pepper.
